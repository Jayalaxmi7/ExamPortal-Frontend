About

Online Examination Portal is a technology-driven way to simplify examination activities like defining exam patterns with question banks, defining exam timers, objective question sections, and conducting exams using a computer in a paperless manner. Online Examination Portal is a cost-effective, scalable way to convert traditional pen and paper-based exams to online and paperless mode. Candidates can appear for the exam using any desktop, laptop with a browser. Exam results can be generated instantly for the objective type of questions. It can simplify overall examination management and result in generation activity.
